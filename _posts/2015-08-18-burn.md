Hand me the lighter.
I need you to burn
as I do.
As we all do.
First hot, fast, passionate.
Then slow, smoldering, distant.

All I see in you is smoke.
All you see in me are embers.
A faint reminder of the warmth we once shared
And the hopes we once held.
