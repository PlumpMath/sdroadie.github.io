Tick.
You keep 
strange hours,
fully alive when I'm
barely awake.

Tock.
Vision 
getting blurry,
streetlights are the only thing
I see
for miles
and miles 
and miles.

This digital clock 
shouldn't 
make a sound.
TICK

I think
I'm almost there,
wherever I was
trying to
TOCK
be.
